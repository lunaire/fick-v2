Clinical Interpretation

For clinical support only. Verify all calculations independently before clinical decisions.

Cardiac Output (CO)

CO = VO₂ / [(SaO₂ − SvO₂) × Hb × 13.4]

Normal: 4–8 L/min  ·  SaO₂, SvO₂ as fractions

O₂ Content (display)

CaO₂ = Hb × 13.4 × SaO₂
CvO₂ = Hb × 13.4 × SvO₂

VO₂ Estimate

< 70 yr: VO₂ = 125 × BSA
≥ 70 yr: VO₂ = 110 × BSA

BSA Formula

BSA = √(Height cm × Weight kg / 3600)

CI = CO / BSA  ·  SV = CO / HR × 1000

Medical Disclaimer & Limitations of Use

For clinical decision support only. FickCalc is a software tool intended to assist — not replace — the clinical judgment of a qualified healthcare professional. Results must always be independently verified before any clinical action is taken.

AI-assisted tool. This application uses automated OCR and algorithmic estimation. Extracted or computed values may contain errors. Always confirm against source documents and laboratory reports.

Not FDA cleared or CE marked. This tool has not been reviewed or approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Health Canada, or any equivalent regulatory body. It is not intended for use as a standalone diagnostic device.

Formula accuracy. Cardiac output is estimated using Fick's principle (CO = VO₂ / [(SaO₂ − SvO₂) × Hb × 13.4]) with VO₂ approximated as 125 × BSA (<70 yr) or 110 × BSA (≥70 yr). These are established clinical estimates, not direct measurements. Actual values may differ based on patient condition, measurement technique, and equipment calibration.

No data transmission. All calculations and OCR processing occur entirely within your browser. No patient data is transmitted to any external server.
